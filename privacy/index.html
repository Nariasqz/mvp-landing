Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Most Valuable Partner ("MVP," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

Account information: Your email address and name when you create an account.

Health data: Period start dates, cycle length, and hormonal life phase you select. This data is stored securely and never sold or shared with third parties.

Usage data: Which moves you mark as done, Field Conditions you set, and app interaction patterns. This is used only to improve your experience.

Survey responses: If you complete the Her Voice survey, your responses and optional contact information are stored securely and used only to improve the app.

What we never do

We never sell your data. We never use your health data for advertising. We never share your information with third parties except as required to operate the app (cloud hosting, push notifications).

How we store it

All data is stored using industry-standard encryption. Health data is stored in our secure database and is accessible only to you and authorized MVP team members for support purposes.

Your rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings. You can request a copy of your data by emailing us at eesingler@gmail.com.

Children

MVP is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes

We'll notify you of material changes to this policy through the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions? Email us at eesingler@gmail.com.
