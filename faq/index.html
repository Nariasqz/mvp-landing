The magic of MVP is in its hormonal phase customization. Every daily action he receives is shaped by where she is in her cycle or life stage. That level of specificity is what makes MVP different from every other relationship app.

Our first version is built for male-female couples because the entire system is designed around one partner's hormonal cycle shaping what the other partner does.

We plan to expand to other relationship types in future versions, and when we do, we want to build those experiences with the same depth and care. Not a quick reskin. A version that actually works for the couples using it.

Each phase of the hormonal cycle affects energy, mood, stress, and what kind of support actually lands. What feels thoughtful during her follicular phase can feel completely tone-deaf during her menstrual phase or luteal phase, when PMS symptoms often occur. The difference is real, and most partners have no way of knowing it.

MVP uses her current phase to shape his daily action so what he does is matched to where she actually is, not just a generic suggestion. For example, during her menstrual phase he might get a comfort-based action focused on reducing her load. During her follicular or ovulatory phase he might get something more energetic or planned.

He does not need to understand the biology. The app translates it for him.

All of her data is hers.

He does not see her cycle history, raw entries, or anything beyond what she has chosen to share. The more she shares, the more personalized his support becomes. That choice is always hers.
