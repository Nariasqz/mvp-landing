Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 28, 2026  ·  Contact: privacy@mostvaluablepartner.app

We built MVP to help partners show up better. That means you trust us with some of the most personal information in your life: your cycle, your health, your relationship. We take that seriously.

This policy explains exactly what we collect, why we collect it, and what we do and don't do with it.

Who we are

Most Valuable Partner (MVP) is an iPhone app developed and operated by Elya Parks, an individual based in the United States. This app is currently available to users in the United States only.

What we collect and why

Everything listed below is linked to your account identity. None of it is used for advertising, cross-app tracking, or any purpose other than running and improving the MVP app.

Your name and email address

We collect the display name and email address you provide when you sign up.

Purpose: App Functionality. Your name personalizes the experience. Your email is used to create your account, authenticate your sessions, and allow account recovery.

Your account ID

When you sign up, our system automatically assigns your account a unique identifier (a UUID). You never see this directly in the app.

Purpose: App Functionality. This ID is how we link all of your data to your account and connect your account to your partner's.

Hormonal phase and cycle data (Her side)

We collect:

  • Your current hormonal life phase: cycling, pregnant, postpartum, breastfeeding, perimenopause, or menopause
  • Period start and end dates you enter manually
  • Estimated cycle length, calculated from your entries
  • Your Field Conditions, a daily signal you set to describe your current mood and energy level

Purpose: App Functionality and Product Personalization. This data is the engine of the app. Your phase and your daily Field Conditions determine which guidance your partner receives each day. The guidance is personalized to your actual state, not a generic template.

This data is treated as health information. See the security section for how we protect it.

Pregnancy, postpartum, and breastfeeding status

If your current phase is pregnancy, postpartum, or breastfeeding, that status is stored as part of your hormonal phase selection.

Purpose: App Functionality and Product Personalization. These are distinct phases with their own guidance needs. Your partner receives moves appropriate to your current situation. This information is treated as sensitive health data and is protected accordingly.

Love languages and onboarding preferences

During onboarding, you can share your primary and secondary love languages and other preferences.

Purpose: Product Personalization. These preferences shape which moves are shown to your partner so the suggestions fit you specifically.

Move completions, streaks, and badge progress (His side)

We track which moves were completed, when they were completed, and the streak and badge milestones that result from that activity.

Purpose: App Functionality and Analytics. Move completions power the streak and badge system. Timestamps on those completions are used to calculate streaks and award badges. We also use this data internally to understand how the product is being used so we can improve it. We do not use a third-party analytics tool. This data stays in our own database and is not shared with anyone.

Partner link

When two accounts link using an invite code, both account IDs are stored together in our database as a couple record.

Purpose: App Functionality. This connection is required for the app to work. It is what allows her phase data and Field Conditions to reach his side of the app.

What we do NOT do with your data

  • We do not sell your data. Not now, not under any future owner, not ever.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing platforms.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites. This app does not participate in cross-app or cross-site tracking of any kind.
  • We do not use your data to target ads anywhere, inside or outside this app.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs inside the app. There is no Mixpanel, Amplitude, Firebase Analytics, or similar tool in the iOS app. Usage data stays in our own database.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not connect to Apple Health or HealthKit. At launch, all cycle dates are entered manually.
  • We do not disclose your data to law enforcement or other parties unless we are legally required to do so by a valid court order or subpoena, in which case we will notify you to the extent permitted by law.

Third-party services that process your data

Two external services handle your app data as part of running the app.

Supabase

Supabase is our database, authentication, and backend infrastructure provider. All app data is stored on Supabase's servers, which run on Amazon Web Services infrastructure. Supabase is a data processor: they store the data we give them and do not use it for any other purpose. Supabase holds SOC 2 Type II certification. You can read their privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

Apple Push Notification Service (APNs)

We use Apple's push notification system to send you notifications. To do this, your device receives a push notification token from Apple. We store that token in our database while your account is active. Push notification payloads never contain your health data, your cycle phase, your Field Conditions, or any other personal information.

This website

This website (mostvaluablepartner.app) uses Google Analytics and the Meta Pixel to understand how visitors find and interact with the site. These tools collect anonymized data about page visits and may use cookies. This website tracking is separate from the iOS app and does not involve your health data, account data, or any information from inside the app.

If you prefer not to be tracked on this website, you can use a browser with tracking protection enabled or install an ad blocker.

What your partner can see

The app is designed to share specific information between partners. Here is exactly what each side can access.

His side can see: her current hormonal phase, her current Field Conditions, and the moves those generate. He cannot see her raw cycle date entries, her full cycle history, her account credentials, or any information the app does not explicitly surface to him.

Her side can see: whether a move he completed landed. She cannot see his account credentials or any data the app does not explicitly surface to her.

These rules are enforced at the database level using Row Level Security policies. If a couple unlinks their accounts, the former partner immediately loses access to data from the other side.

How we protect your data

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and TLS.
  • All data is encrypted at rest in Supabase's infrastructure.
  • Database access is governed by Row Level Security policies on every table. These are technical controls that prevent any account from reading data it is not authorized to see.
  • Push notification content is intentionally minimal and never includes health data.
  • Individual user data is not reviewed manually as part of normal operations.

No security system is perfect. We have taken reasonable steps to protect your data, but we cannot guarantee that unauthorized access will never occur. If we become aware of a breach affecting your data, we will notify you promptly.

How long we keep your data

Your data is kept for as long as your account is active. Specific retention rules:

  • Account deletion: When you delete your account, all of your data is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days. This cannot be undone.
  • Cycle history: Cycle date entries older than 90 days are automatically deleted from active accounts. Only recent data is needed to determine your current phase.
  • Field Conditions: Only your most recent Field Conditions entry is kept. History is not retained beyond 24 hours.

Your rights and choices

  • Edit your data: You can update your profile, phase, cycle entries, Field Conditions, and preferences at any time from within the app.
  • Delete your account: You can delete your account directly in the app. All data is permanently removed within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.
  • Turn off notifications: You can disable push notifications in your iPhone Settings at any time.
  • Request a copy of your data or ask a question: Email privacy@mostvaluablepartner.app. We will respond within 7 business days.

Children

MVP is not directed at children. You must be 17 or older to use this app. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 17. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us at privacy@mostvaluablepartner.app and we will remove it promptly.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you in the app before those changes take effect. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version. We will not change how we use your data without telling you first.
